Detailed Description

The role of the ContactListener (CL) is to listen for collisions and call the appropriate functions as defined by the user. Typically, the CL will be attached to a gameobject that has a collider. A rigidbody is not necessary per se, but it'll likely be needed for non-trigger collision response.
